Umrah with My Mother

This Journey start at April 2019. My mom and I decided want to go to umrah. It is first time for me to go Umrah.

When I in front of door to enter the Masjidil Haram, My mind said not everyone is able to go this place and very grateful for it because able to go there.

One sentence that make me feel grateful from the guider(Mutawif). "Kamu nampak kaabah di hadapan kamu, jika kamu nampak itu maknanya Allah meredhoi kedatangan kamu ke sini kerana ada yang tidak dapat melihatnya di depan mata mereka." It make me feel so overwhelmed that can not be expresed.

I pray that those who want to come here will be granted their intentions
